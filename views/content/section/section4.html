Just When You Thought, We Were Done!

Never Grow Up is proud to be associated with clients to execute their CSR Initiatives. Besides empowering employees, we have taken up initiatives to touch the lives of individuals in society as well.

We believe in doing our bit. Never Grow Up ® in association with the Smile Foundation has run Social Media campaigns which helped generate funds to support areas like children’s education and health for the NGO.

We have also run activity based ‘story telling’ workshops for underprivileged kids with the Bombay Community Public Trust that provides for the promotion and funding of institutions and welfare agencies involved in public works and addressing environmental problems among other areas.

Never Grow Up has also been the corporate engagement partner for Yes Foundation’s YES! I Am the Change, India's largest Social Film making challenge which aims to inspire, engage and empower people to make a 4 minute film in 100 hours pertaining to social issues.
