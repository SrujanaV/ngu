Our Work

At Never Grow Up ®, we believe in letting our work speak for ourselves. You may not hear us blowing the trumpet too loud, but that’s just the way we are. Over the last few years, we have worked on a variety of projects across industries & levels while being true to our philosophy of making work fun, keeping people engaged and helping organizations retain and hone talent. Here’s a peek into some of our work.

From getting every employee of Tata Sky believe and feel like a CEO to executing a Competency Project by anchoring behaviours through a creative visual campaign, we’ve partnered with Tata Sky over initiatives that have witnessed PAN-India impact. Besides these campaigns, we have also designed their Values & Culture Handbook with easy-to-understand infographics and we are just getting started.

With the objective of inculcating a sense of pride among employees in the logistics business, we partnered with DGF to create a three phase ‘Passionate Forwarder’ campaign. Across major cities in India, interactive workshops for the employees were executed with every Forwarder receiving a customised Pride Package that contained goodies specially made in line with the theme.

Be it facilitating a Brainstorming session for their employees through office branding or handling the entire back-end operations of their media launch for the Sharing Beauty with All program, we have seamlessly joined hands with L’Oréal in their endeavours. Besides being creative partners, the occasion of their annual Citizen's Day, a day where all the employees of L’Oréal commit to make a difference to the society they live in, saw us managing NGO partnerships across locations.

Be it conducting theatre-based workshops to spread awareness on Gender Diversity and Sensitivity for their employees or celebrating the International Girl Child Day by organizing workshops for pre-teens from low-income communities, Never Grow Up has been associated with ABFSG to develop ideas and implement them across several initiatives besides building communication for various internal initiatives.

At ICICI Prudential, the spirit and belief of service excellence is rewarded on an annual basis through an internal initiative known as 'Tatva'. Never Grow Up has partnered with them to plan and execute the roll-out of this entire campaign for over two years. We have also implemented a series of monthly internal campaigns to sustain the buzz around Tatva while keeping the program aspirational.

For the vibrant bunch that they are, we have conceptualised and implemented an extremely innovative and relevant Induction program for over 350 of its new joinees till date. By conducting a customized experiential program spread over days, their motto of 'Look Up' has been executed effortlessly across levels and roles.

With the core objective of reliving and imbibing company values across the organization and adding value to Employee Engagement practices Never Grow Up ® partnered with Shoppers Stop to launch a 3 month initiative called 'IPledge'. While the idea meant to reach out to every single person across the organization brought a new sense of camaraderie across employees and stores, this initiative also got our client into the Limca Book of Records. This was followed by an award for 'The organization with the most Innovative HR Practices' award for IPledge at the World HRD Congress 'Talent and HR Leadership Awards. That's not all! Another initiative aptly called the 'Goodie Box' TM conceptualized and created by our team has also helped them bag the award for 'The most Innovative HR initiative' at the award ceremony. The program lasted two years with evolutionary steps being taken to sustain and enhance the campaign.

As a case in point, we have worked on a series of turnkey initiatives ranging from Internal Communication Campaigns, key policy roll-outs, Reward and Recognition Programs, helping launch the Innovation Platform for the company, making Campus Presentations, running Corporate Social Responsibility Campaigns, Idea Generation Workshops, customized Training Interventions, designing Staff Welcome Kits while managing ‘Fun Fridays’! We’ve also helped roll out the company Sexual Harassment Policy, a pilot ‘Counselling Centre’ for employees and worked closely with the team to rollout their new dress code and create an ‘Original Song’ dedicated to Retail Employees across the country.

For Groupm, Mindshare & Maxus, we have created and creatively executed customized, activity led interventions across levels – ranging from Senior Management to a new entrant. These have been in sync with team meets, strategy discussions, off-sites and have met pre-defined objectives. We’ve also helped design a couple of meeting rooms for the creative bunch that they are! What fun!
